The house rules

What we will and won’t show.

Every drink is drawn from an official menu, with the date we checked it. Recipes vary by location.

No ratings, reviews, wait times, or prices we haven’t verified. Unknown stays unknown.

Our selections are opinions, not placements. Any sponsored item would be labelled plainly.
