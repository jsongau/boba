Why trust this

How CapyBoba stays right

Verified, and dated

Hours and open-or-closed status come from each shop's own listing, with the date we checked shown on the page. A wrong hour that sends you to a closed shop is the one thing we will not do.

Ranked by fit, not a fake score

Our best-for lists are opinions with the method shown. We rank on fit for the moment, date night, study, late night, never a made-up quality rating, and never a paid one.

Openings and closures, tracked

We watch for new shops and closures every few weeks, so the list keeps up instead of rotting into an old screenshot you can't trust.

How we make money: shops can pay for a membership and featured placement, and that funds the directory that sends them customers. It never buys a better ranking on a best-for list, and it never buys a review.
