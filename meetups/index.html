The social calendar

Meetups & clubs that run on boba

Southern California treats boba shops as third places — clubs meet in them, crawls route through them, and fandoms take them over for a night. Everything below is a real group with a real public page; details come from each organizer's own listing, so check their page before you go.

Sourced We list only what an organizer publishes, and we link straight to them. Days and venues change — the organizer's page is always the truth. Nothing here is sponsored.

Boba social clubs

The 626Social club

SoCal Boba Run Club

San Gabriel Valley & beyond · boba crawls, mahjong nights, sober raves

The flagship. Their words: "no running, just vibes." Launched at 3CAT in Arcadia and now fills tea bars across the 626 — Summerfield Tea Bar in Rosemead, UG Tea in San Gabriel, Jianghui NLA in Alhambra — with events that draw a few hundred people.

Follow: @socalbobarunclub · events post via Partiful

The 626Game night

SGV Boba Mahjong Club

Summerfield Tea Bar · 9021 Valley Blvd, Rosemead

Mahjong nights inside a working tea bar — recent editions ran Saturday evenings. Bring patience; the tiles move fast.

Recent listing: Partiful

Greater LASocial club

Boba Buddies LA

Westwood, Santa Monica, K-town · via Meetup

The format is simple: order at a shop — past picks include Junbi, Just Boba, and Sunright — then find a spot nearby and actually talk. Sometimes board games.

Join: meetup.com/boba-buddies-la

San DiegoSocial club

Boba Tea Social Club

Mira Mesa & Clairemont Mesa · roughly monthly

Two-thousand-plus members meeting at the shops along the Convoy corridor's orbit — Cafe 86 on Mira Mesa Blvd, Tea N More on Clairemont Mesa Blvd.

Join: meetup.com/bobateasocial

Orange CountyBook club

Silent Book Club — Irvine

One Zo Boba · Woodbury Town Center, Irvine · Thursdays per their page

Order a drink, read silently for forty-five minutes with a few thousand fellow members' chapter, then talk about it. The rare club where showing up alone is the whole point.

Join: meetup.com/silent-book-club-irvine

Crawls, runs & circuits

PasadenaCrawl

Pasadena Boba Trail

29 shops · Old Pasadena, Playhouse Village, South Lake

The city's official digital passport — check in at shops along Route 66, collect points, earn boba-themed prizes. A ready-made route for a group afternoon or an ambitious second date.

Start: visitpasadena.com

AnywhereRun

Run 4 Boba 5K

Virtual · next edition listed for late August per the race page

The one entry where running is actually involved: finish your 5K anywhere, then — their words — reward yourself with boba from your favorite shop.

Enter: raceentry.com

Fandom nights

Greater LA + OCCupsleeve

The K-pop cupsleeve circuit

DOL Cafe (Gardena) and Krak Boba (Lomita) are documented repeat hosts

Fan-organized celebrations around idol birthdays and comebacks: collectible cup sleeves with any drink, artist alley, raffles. The circuit also passes through Katsubo Tea in Fullerton, Almond Haus in Garden Grove, Feng Cha in Costa Mesa, Calitea in Long Beach, and Miss Cheese Tea in Pasadena.

Track events: kpoptracker.net · background: Spectrum News

Little TokyoCollab

Sunright Tea Studio collab meetups

Official game & anime collaborations with in-store gatherings

The chain runs sanctioned crossovers — Honkai Star Rail with voice actors and cosplayers, MapleStory pop-ups — that turn the Little Tokyo store into a fan meetup for a weekend.

Current collabs: snrtea.com/collaboration

If yours does (or you're a shop that wants to host one), we'll list it with a link to your page. Tell us here — listing is free, and we only publish what your own page says.

FAQ

How do we know these are real?

Every listing links to the organizer's own public page — Meetup, Instagram, Partiful, or an official site. We publish nothing we can't link.

Are the days and times current?

Schedules change without telling us, so we point you at the organizer's page instead of asserting times ourselves. When we say "Thursdays," it's because their page said so at our last check.

Meeting someone from an app instead?

Different mission, same drinks — start with the First Meet guide on our homepage, then pick a first-meet room.
