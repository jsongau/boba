About

What CapyBoba is

CapyBoba is a directory of boba shops across Southern California, built around one idea: most boba runs start with a moment, not a brand.

You don't wake up wanting a specific chain. You want somewhere close for a first date, a table to study at until it closes, a fruit tea that uses real fruit, or a drive-thru on the way home. CapyBoba is organized around those moments, then points you to real shops that fit.

What we cover

We're tracking hundreds of shops across five areas: the San Gabriel Valley, Greater Los Angeles, Orange County, San Diego, and the Inland Empire. Every listing starts with a confirmed name and address, then fills in hours, location, and details as we verify them against each shop's own information.

What we won't do

We don't invent ratings, reviews, or rankings. Third-party ratings, when shown, are pulled live and attributed to their source, never stored and passed off as ours. Our best-for lists are clearly labeled opinions with the method shown, and no shop can pay for a spot on them. More in how we rank and how we make money.

For shop owners

Listings are free. If it's your shop, you can claim it to keep your hours and details correct. See something wrong on any page? Report it and we'll check.
