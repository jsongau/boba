For shop owners

Claim your shop

If it's your boba shop, claim the listing to keep your hours, menu, and details correct.

What claiming does

  • Correct your hours, address, and phone.
  • Add your signature drinks and amenities.
  • Show a Claimed badge so customers know the info is from you.

How to claim

Email claim@niteboba.vercel.app from an address tied to the business, with your shop name and city and your role. We'll verify and set you up.

Claiming is free. Paid membership options for shops that want more presence are being finalized, and any paid placement is labeled. See how we make money.
