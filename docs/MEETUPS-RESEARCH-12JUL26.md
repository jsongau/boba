# Meetups & Run Clubs at SoCal Boba Shops — research findings (12 JUL 2026)

Four parallel research agents swept web/Meetup/Partiful/Instagram-mirrors/news.
Every claim below carries a source URL. Confidence reflects what a public
source actually states — nothing here is guessed. This feeds `/meetups/` and
the Supabase `niteboba_meetups` table.

## The headline finding (read this first)

**"Boba run club" in SoCal does not mean jogging.** The flagship —
**SoCal Boba Run Club** (@socalbobarunclub, ~7.2K IG) — says it outright:
"no running, just vibes. We do boba runs, mahjong clubs, and sober raves."
Launched April 2025 at 3CAT (Arcadia); 18+ events since, averaging 200–500
people, hosted AT boba/tea shops: 3CAT (Arcadia), Summerfield Tea Bar
(Rosemead), UG Tea (San Gabriel), Jianghui NLA (Alhambra).
Source: https://www.instagram.com/socalbobarunclub/ · launch reel:
https://www.instagram.com/reel/DIH10GyyboK/ (high confidence)

Meanwhile, actual jogging clubs in the 626 (MPK Run Club, Ohana Running Crew,
Renaissance in Motion, Pasadena Run Club) publish **coffee and beer ties, zero
boba partnerships** — checked individually. **The run-club × boba-shop lane is
genuinely unclaimed.** That's a NiteBoba opportunity, not a listing.

## First-date validation (feeds the First Meet module)

- The boba first date is a named Gen Z pattern (TikTok discover hubs "Boba
  Tea First Date"; Lemon8 guides). tiktok.com/discover/boba-tea-first-date
- ~72% of Americans prefer coffee-shop-style first-date venues; 68% cite low
  pressure (gitnux.org/first-date-statistics — med confidence).
- eharmony + Bumble's own guides codify the venue checklist: quiet enough to
  talk, real seating, ~45–60 min, "cut short or extend" escape hatch, public.
- Backlash exists: bare drink dates can read low-effort → light programming
  (icebreakers, mini-itinerary) is the fix. That's exactly the Pearl Deck +
  First Meet module. Card-deck reference format: We're Not Really Strangers
  (150 cards, 3 escalating levels).
- AI date-itinerary generators exist (4Lovebirds, PlanMyDate.ai) but NONE are
  boba-specific — open lane for the directory-powered planner.
