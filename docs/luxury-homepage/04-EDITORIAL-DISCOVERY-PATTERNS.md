# 04 - Editorial Discovery and City-Guide Patterns

Method: synthesis from established knowledge of The Infatuation, Michelin Guide, Beli, World of Mouth, Resy and OpenTable editorial, Tock, and independent city guides. No third-party screenshots captured here.

- **Strong recommendations:** a named pick with a one-line reason beats a star average. -> The House Edit names three picks (classic / modern / unexpected) with one reason each, labelled as opinion.
- **Editorial, not algorithmic:** human titles, short standfirsts, a point of view, dates. -> "Where the good rooms are," "Freshly checked."
- **The item is the hero:** the drink leads, the venue is context.
- **Save and send:** lightweight lists and share links, no account. -> The Black Book + Web Share with clipboard fallback.
- **Location stays practical:** menu and directions one tap away; hours only when verified.
- **Trust without fake scores:** show sources and dates, label sponsorship, invite corrections. -> The "house rules" section.
