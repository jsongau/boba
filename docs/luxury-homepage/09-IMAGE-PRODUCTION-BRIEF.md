# 09 - Image Production Brief

Licensed photography could not be sourced in this environment (no rights-cleared assets, and this build will not rehost or pass off unlicensed images as real menu items). The homepage therefore ships with owned, art-directed CSS/SVG placeholders, clearly labelled "editorial rendering." This brief specifies the real assets to commission or shoot to replace them. Consistent lighting language throughout: deep blacks, one warm key light, condensation, glass, tea color, cream, tactile ingredients. Reserve dimensions to prevent layout shift; ship AVIF/WebP with fallbacks; write meaningful alt text; never present an editorial mood image as a specific real menu item.

1. **Macro drink hero** (arrival). Smoked-glass vessel, deep oolong tea, a cream ribbon curling in, pearls settling, condensation, warm rim light on near-black. 100mm macro, f4. Desktop 5:6 portrait; mobile 4:5. Editorial.
2. **Pouring tea.** Milk streaming into tea from above, motion frozen, steam. 85mm, f2.8. 16:10. Editorial.
3. **Tapioca pearls and texture.** Extreme macro of glossy pearls, brown-sugar sheen. 100mm macro. Square. Editorial.
4. **Hands holding an elevated drink.** Two hands, warm skin, matte cup, blurred night interior. 50mm, f1.8. 4:5. Editorial.
5. **Evening storefront.** A real tea house at dusk, warm window light, wet pavement reflection. 35mm. 16:10. Editorial (not a specific endorsement).
6. **Modern tea interior.** Quiet counter, ceramic, warm wood, low light. 24mm. 3:2. Editorial.
7. **Date-night moment.** Two people, two drinks, candor not posing, bokeh. 50mm. 4:5. Editorial.
8. **Ingredient still life.** Loose tea leaves, taro, matcha, brown sugar on dark stone. 60mm macro. 3:2. Editorial.
9. **City atmosphere.** Convoy or SGV boulevard at night, signage bokeh. 35mm. 16:9. Editorial.
10. **Menu object.** A paper menu or receipt on a dark table, shallow focus. 50mm. 3:2. May depict a real menu only if photographed with permission; otherwise keep generic.

Each asset maps to a slot in data/research/asset-ledger.csv. Until shot, the CSS/SVG placeholders carry the visual language so the hero is never blank.
