# 07 - Three Luxury Concepts

Each concept carries emotional promise, palette, type, image style, hero, interactive centerpiece, section order, mobile, mega-nav, share card, data needs, and risks, plus a score.

## Concept A - The Pearl Room (cinematic private tasting room)
- **Promise:** invited into a quiet nocturnal room and handed exactly the right drink.
- **Palette:** obsidian, smoked, pearl, porcelain, champagne (thin accent), deep jade, garnet, aubergine.
- **Type:** Fraunces (sparingly) + Inter; champagne small-caps labels.
- **Image:** macro liquid, condensation, pearls, warm rim light on dark; porcelain relief.
- **Hero:** asymmetric split, macro Pour + wordmark + one line + Tonight's Selection note.
- **Centerpiece:** the Tea Concierge (four questions -> one sourced selection).
- **Order:** arrival, concierge, house edit, house choice, tasting flight, city after dark, new pour, evening routes, black book, guides, directory, trust.
- **Mobile:** Pour + note in first screen; details-drawer nav.
- **Mega-nav:** concierge index with a featured plate.
- **Share card:** a plain-text note (drink, shop, city) via Web Share.
- **Data:** sourced drinks + chain locations (have).
- **Perf risk:** low (CSS/SVG art, two fonts). **A11y risk:** dark contrast must stay AA (validated).
- **Why expensive:** restraint, macro material, editorial pacing, one signature.
- **Could feel fake if:** it drifts to black+gold-only or passes a rendering off as a real photo. Mitigated by jade/garnet/porcelain moments and "editorial rendering" labels.

## Concept B - Tea House Moderne (brighter daylight salon)
- **Promise:** a calm contemporary tea salon in daylight; paper menus, ritual.
- **Palette:** warm limestone, porcelain, lacquer red, soft jade/sage; dark only for type.
- **Type:** a sculptural serif + humanist sans; more air, larger type.
- **Image:** bright soft-shadow still life; paper and ceramic.
- **Hero:** left editorial with a bright macro cup and a paper-menu motif.
- **Centerpiece:** the same concierge on paper cards.
- **Order:** as A but lighter, paper-menu feel. **Mobile:** bright, generous, paper cards stack.
- **Mega-nav:** light paper index. **Share:** paper-styled note. **Data:** same.
- **Perf risk:** low. **A11y risk:** light red on limestone can fail AA; needs care.
- **Why expensive:** daylight restraint, ceramic materiality.
- **Could feel fake if:** it tips into the common cream + serif + terracotta default.

## Concept C - Boba Society After Dark (expressive nightlife editorial)
- **Promise:** a fashion-forward night out; a private black book of tea plans.
- **Palette:** black, ivory, oxblood, jewel accents; flash-lit energy.
- **Type:** cropped editorial display, condensed labels; stronger contrast.
- **Image:** flash photography, city nightlife, cropped hands and cups.
- **Hero:** full-bleed flash image with cropped type overlay.
- **Centerpiece:** the concierge as a nightlife "host"; routes and the black book are the stars.
- **Order:** arrival, routes, concierge, flight, black book, city, directory, trust. **Mobile:** image-led, bold.
- **Mega-nav:** editorial, high-contrast. **Share:** a bold plan card. **Data:** same, plus stronger city imagery.
- **Perf risk:** medium-high (flash imagery wants real photos). **A11y risk:** high-contrast + cropped type can hurt legibility.
- **Why expensive:** fashion energy, editorial crop.
- **Could feel fake if:** it relies on the near-black + single acid-accent default, or on unlicensed flash photos we cannot source.
