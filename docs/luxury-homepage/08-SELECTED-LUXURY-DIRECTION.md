# 08 - Selected Direction: CapyBoba Society, The Pearl Room

## Why this direction
It reaches the required quality bar while staying feasible and honest: the signature is an owned art-directed render, the palette avoids the black+gold cliché through porcelain relief and jade/garnet/aubergine moments, and every recommendation is real and sourced.

## Palette (validated, WCAG AA reasoned per surface)
obsidian #0B0C0E, smoked #17191D, pearl #F4EFE7, porcelain #FCF9F3, champagne #C5A46D (thin accent + labels only), old-gold #8E734B, deep-jade #123F35, garnet #7A2433, aubergine #342235, silver #C8C6C1, ink #151619, muted #76736D. Body text: pearl/silver on dark (>=11:1), ink on porcelain. Champagne is used for labels and large accents only, never as body text on dark.

## Typography
Display: Fraunces, used sparingly for the headline and section titles, italic for one accent word. Body/UI: Inter. Labels/index: Inter uppercase, tracked, champagne. Space Grotesk and Inter Tight removed.

## Signature
The Pour: an art-directed SVG rendering of tea poured over settling pearls in a smoked-glass vessel, with a cream ribbon, condensation, and a rim of light. It anchors the hero and recurs as textural "plate" fields. It is an editorial rendering, labelled as such, never a photo of a specific menu item.

## Composition and rhythm
Obsidian sections alternate with porcelain reading panels; deep jade for the concierge, aubergine for the flight, garnet accents in the New Pour. Content forms: concierge note, menu sheet (hairline rows), flight columns, full-bleed city split, magazine list, route card, notebook, restrained directory entry. Corners 3 to 6px; hairline dividers; large fields; occasional pill only for a true control.

## Section order (implemented)
1 Arrival / Tonight's Selection, 2 Tea Concierge, 3 The House Edit, 4 House Choice, 5 The Tasting Flight, 6 City After Dark, 7 The New Pour, 8 An Evening In, 9 The Black Book, 10 The House Guide, 11 Society Directory, 12 Trust and sources.

## Navigation
Top level: Tonight, The Concierge, Cities, Tea Houses, The Menu, Guides, The New Pour, Black Book. Five expand into a concierge-index panel (featured plate + grouped anchor links + subtle index numbers); three are direct links. Mobile is a full-height drawer with search and recent selections.

## Motion
Soft reveal on scroll (progressive enhancement only), soft fades on concierge steps, a gentle turn on the House Choice reveal. No floating pearls, no wheel, no scroll hijack, no loader. Fully disabled under prefers-reduced-motion, with content still visible.

## Copy
Informed, sensual, concise: "Something exquisite for tonight," "A more considered way to choose boba," "Leave it to the house," "Keep it," "Send the evening." No arcade language, no "luxury/exclusive/VIP" self-description, no em or en dashes as punctuation.
