The Ritual
Four minutes to a perfect pour

Cheese foam is not a topping. It is the finish. Here is how the good rooms build it after dark.

01
Brew slow, brew dark

Assam and oolong steeped long enough to hold their ground under cream.

02
Whip the foam cold

Cream cheese, sea salt, a whisper of sugar, whipped until it holds a cloud.

03
Sink the pearls

Tapioca cooked to order, still warm, resting at the bottom like river stones.

04
Sip at an angle

Tilt, do not stir. Let the salted cloud meet the tea on the way down.
