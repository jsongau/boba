The House Ritual

How a boutique picks a pour.

  1. The tea, first

    Loose leaf and single origin over powder. If the base does not stand alone, the shop does not make the shelf.

  2. The pearl, second

    Cooked to order, still warm, with a bite that holds. No tin earns a label on a cold, tired pearl.

  3. The room, always

    Late hours, low light, a place worth lingering. A pour is only half the evening.
