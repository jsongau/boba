## Details

### 01 · Midnight Atelier

A quiet-luxury, editorial-print take on Boba Night where obsidian, gold hairlines, and a huge restrained Fraunces headline turn boba discovery into an after-dark atlas that surfaces the closest good cup first.

- **Hero:** Full-viewport obsidian canvas with a soft off-screen gold radial glow. A gold hairline kicker reads "Southern California — After Dark", above an oversized light-weight Fraunces headline "The city goes quiet. The boba stays open." with "boba" set in gold italic. A thin rule separates the headline from a two-part footer: a muted lede on the left and a pearl pill CTA "Find boba near me" with a gold dot on the right. Generous whitespace, single gold accent, magazine restraint throughout.
- **Signature interaction:** A proximity finder ("The Atlas") built from Supabase-style city data: pick a SoCal corner (Rowland Heights, Cypress, Irvine, Arcadia, Koreatown) via gold pill chips, and the panel re-renders the boba rooms ordered nearest-first, each row staggering in with a fade-and-rise, showing rank, shop name in Fraunces, a detail line, and a distance in miles. Directly answers the project brief of finding the closest boba by city.
- **Palette:** Obsidian #0B0C0E and a slightly lifted #101215 for panels, pearl #F4EFE7 text, champagne/gold #C5A46D as the single accent (hairlines, italics, chips, glow), with muted pearl at 52% and gold/pearl low-opacity hairline borders. No orchid used. Fraunces (display) + Inter (UI).
- **File:** `concepts/01-midnight-atelier.html`

### 02 · Neon Night Market

A dark, after-dark SoCal boba homepage that channels Taiwanese night-market energy through controlled neon pink and jade glow, hand-drawn signage rhythm, and a live nearest-stall finder.

- **Hero:** Obsidian canvas washed with soft pink/jade/orchid haze and film grain. A giant Fraunces headline "The night market is open, find your boba" where the word "open" burns as flickering neon pink text with layered glow, and "find your boba" set in gold italic. A pulsing lantern dot sits in the wordmark. A glowing pink pill CTA "Find boba near me" plus a jade-outline ghost CTA sit below a calm subhead.
- **Signature interaction:** A city chip row (Rowland Heights, Cypress, Irvine, Arcadia) drives a live "closest first" stall grid: tapping a neighborhood re-sorts all sample SoCal shops by real per-city distance values, re-staggers their rise-in animation, relabels each card's distance (under 1 mi vs N mi away), and highlights the active chip in glowing neon pink. A secondary scrolling neon marquee of night-market phrases runs continuously.
- **Palette:** Obsidian #0B0C0E base, pearl #F4EFE7 text, champagne gold #C5A46D accents, jade #123F35 bands, orchid #b46bd6 for featured stalls only, plus a controlled neon pink #ff5c9d and neon jade #3ff0b8 for glow.
- **File:** `concepts/02-neon-night-market.html`
