About Taro Yuan is Taiwan's taro specialist — a dessert house built around fresh-made taro balls rather than a standard chain menu. The Gale Avenue shop brings the original Taiwanese lineup to the San Gabriel Valley: signature ice, warm soups in winter, and taro in nearly everything.
